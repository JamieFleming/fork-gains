Caprese Chicken Salad

A refreshing salad featuring grilled chicken, fresh mozzarella, tomatoes, and basil.

  • Serves: 4
  • Calories: 875 kcal
  • Protein: 101g
  • Carbs: 8g
  • Fat: 44g
  • Cooking Time: 20 minutes

Ingredients

  • 500g Dry Pasta
  • 2 Chicken Breasts
  • 1 Ball of Mozzarella
  • Handful of Cherry Tomatoes, halved
  • 1 Red Bell Pepper, sliced
  • 1 Red onion, sliced
  • 1/2 Cucumber, sliced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • Handful of capers
  • Handful of fresh basil leaves
  • 1 tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • 1 Lemon
  • Salt and Pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Boil a large pot of salted water and cook the pasta according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper. Cook in air fryer at 200Â°C for 15-20 minutes until fully cooked. Let rest for a few minutes, then slice into strips.
  3. Slice the tomatoes, red bell pepper, and red onion into slices.
  4. In a large bowl, combine the cooked pasta, sliced chicken, tomatoes, bell pepper, red onion, cucumber, crushed garlic, capers, and fresh basil leaves.
  5. Roughly chop the mozzarella ball and add it to the bowl.
  6. Drizzle with olive oil and squeeze the juice of one lemon over the salad. Toss to combine.
  7. Season with additional salt and pepper to taste. Serve immediately or refrigerate for later.
