Creamy Cajun Pasta

A rich and spicy pasta dish with a creamy sauce infused with cajun spices.

  • Serves: 5
  • Calories: 980 kcal
  • Protein: 35g
  • Carbs: 79g
  • Fat: 9g
  • Cooking Time: 30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 500g Dry Pasta
  • 500g Chicken Breast
  • 1 Red Onion
  • 200g Mushrooms
  • 1 Red Bell Pepper
  • 2 Cloves of Garlic
  • 1 tbsp Cajun Seasoning
  • 1 tbsp Olive Oil
  • 100g Light Cream Cheese
  • 500ml Chicken Stock
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
  • Chives for garnish

Instructions

  1. Boil a large pot of salted water and cook the pasta according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper. Cook in air fryer at 200°C for 15-20 minutes until fully cooked. Let rest for a few minutes, then slice into strips.
  3. Slice the red onion, mushrooms, and red bell pepper into thin slices.
  4. Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add sliced onions, mushrooms, and bell peppers. Sauté for 5-7 minutes until softened.
  5. Add cajun seasoning and garlic to the pan. Cook for another minute until fragrant.
  6. Stir in cream cheese and chicken stock. Simmer for 5 minutes until sauce thickens.
  7. Combine the cooked pasta with the sauce. Toss to coat evenly.
  8. Garnish with chives before serving.
  9. Serve hot and enjoy your creamy cajun pasta or portion and refrigerate any leftovers.
